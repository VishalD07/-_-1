Tribute to Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward. He is considered one of the greatest footballers of all time.

Achievements

  • 5 Ballon d'Or awards
  • 4 European Golden Shoes
  • 7 League titles
  • 5 UEFA Champions League titles
  • 1 UEFA European Championship
  • 1 UEFA Nations League
  • Over 800 career goals
